How It Works

A modern NLP pipeline from raw text to decision

01
Text Preprocessing

Raw text is lowercased, stripped of URLs, punctuation, and stop words to produce a clean token stream.

02
TF-IDF Vectorisation

Text is converted into a high-dimensional numerical vector (10,000 features, unigrams + bigrams) weighting term importance.

03
ML Classification

Three models compete (Logistic Regression, Naïve Bayes, LinearSVC). The best by Macro F1 is deployed for real-time inference.
